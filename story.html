My Story

My Macro Coach started as a simple idea: a macornutrient tracking app that educates the user around healthy eating habbits and nutrition.

What is begining as a solo project will hopefully grow into a large community effort. Having this amount of community will help both in the developmenty of My Macro Coach but also in the evergrowing feature list.

We believe that tracking macros should be intuitive, customizable, and healthy. As we continue to grow, we welcome everyone to contribute, whether by offering feedback, suggesting new features, or collaborating on the github.

Thank you for being part of our journey!
